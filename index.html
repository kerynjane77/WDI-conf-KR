Join us for 11 short tech talks at General Assembly!

Speakers & talks

Minecraft Ninja

Ashton Sobell

Secrets to getting the ultimate high score, building your own palace pyramid, and catching all the pigs for your own farm.

10 tips for work

Aviel Goh

Aviel will talk about his experiences and tips on working in a corporate organisation.

Extreme barista

Ivan Yapeter

Ivan brings his signature crazy style to the technology and philosophy of fine coffee sourcing, brewing, and tasting.

Life Model From Waterfall to Agile

Bono Liu

Spending 50% of your time on planning and design or get progressing every sprint? That's your choice!

How to hack a website

Asep Norzai

Spending 50% of your time on planning and design or get progressing every sprint? That's your choice!

The art of procrastination

Helen Habakuk

Helen demonstrates her app, which makes procrastinating much more fun and takes away any guilt from not being productive.

Code a 'Jumpy Piranha' game

Gregory Fernandes

Greg will demo how to code a game like Flappy Bird (which earned the creator around $50,000 per day). The demo take 10 minutes, but will get you rolling like a cannon ball.

Hacking for humanity

Keryn Robinson

Keryn will talk about her first experience at a weekend hackathon, working with a team for a social enterprise called Right Click Community.

Ravi blue – css tricks

Ravi Chada

But when you have to turn into a chrysalis—you will some day, you know—and then after that into a butterfly

Having an open mind

Kylie Sy

How to stay humble, make the most of opportunities, and keep learning your whole life.

Rationality

Toby Letcher

Toby introduces several theories of decision making and heuristics. He will cover cognitive fallacies and behavioral economics.
